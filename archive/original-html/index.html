Our story

Built on experience.

Nearly four decades of healthcare design experience, shaped by practical site knowledge and long-term client relationships.

Established 1987
Hong Kong / Singapore / PRC

Since 1987, Hallwicks Design Limited has been creating thoughtful interior design solutions for medical and dental clinics across Hong Kong, Singapore, and the PRC. With more than 38 years of experience, we understand that healthcare spaces need to do more than look refined. They must support clinical workflow, patient comfort, staff efficiency, and long-term durability.

Our work combines design expertise with practical delivery support. Alongside our design team, we work with trusted manufacturing and site teams, including furniture production, painting, steelwork, glasswork, and lighting specialists. This allows us to provide a comprehensive service from concept to completion, helping each project achieve a high standard of function, detail, and atmosphere.
