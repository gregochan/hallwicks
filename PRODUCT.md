# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective healthcare operators, clinic founders, dental groups, specialist medical teams, and project stakeholders in Hong Kong, China, and Singapore who need a credible interior architecture partner for clinical environments.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Hallwicks Design Limited as a focused clinical and medical interior architecture studio with long-standing regional experience. Success means visitors quickly understand the studio's authority, care-environment expertise, selected works, and how to begin a project conversation.

## Brand Personality

Precise, established, disciplined. The experience should feel architectural, clinical, confident, and quietly premium without becoming decorative or corporate-generic.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic agency landing pages, soft SaaS gradients, stock healthcare imagery, decorative glass cards, and content-heavy legacy brochure layouts. The site should not feel like a template, a hospital administration portal, or a wellness brand.

## Design Principles

- Make expertise visible through specificity: clinical workflows, specialist environments, regional experience, and built project evidence.
- Use restraint as authority: monochrome structure, disciplined spacing, and selective Hallwicks green accents.
- Let photography carry proof: project imagery should show real interiors and material detail.
- Keep motion architectural: scroll effects should clarify depth and sequence, not distract from the work.
- Make content editable without weakening the art direction: CMS fields should map to curated, intentional frontend components.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for readable text and controls. Maintain reduced-motion paths for GSAP and Lenis interactions. Preserve keyboard navigation, semantic sections, meaningful image alt text, and non-color-only interaction cues.
